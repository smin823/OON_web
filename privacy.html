Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 06, 2026

Plolabs (hereinafter referred to as "Company") complies with applicable privacy laws and regulations, including personal information protection laws, and establishes and discloses the following privacy policy to protect users' personal information.

This Privacy Policy applies to the OON app service.

1. Purpose of Collection and Use of Personal Information

The Company collects and uses personal information for the following purposes:

  • Providing services including daily fortune, tarot, constellation fortune, and zodiac fortune
  • Providing personalized content to users
  • Service operation and quality improvement

2. Personal Information Items Collected

The Company collects the following personal information to provide services:

1) Information directly entered by users

  • Name
  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Time of birth

* The above information is stored on our servers.

2) Information automatically collected

  • Advertising ID
  • App usage logs, access records, device information
  • Service usage records and error logs

3. Method of Personal Information Collection

  • Direct input by users during initial app launch or service use
  • Automatic collection of information generated during app use (logs, advertising ID, etc.)

4. Retention and Use Period of Personal Information

The Company promptly destroys personal information after the purpose of collection and use has been achieved.
However, information is retained and automatically deleted according to the following criteria:

  • Inactive status for 12 months after last use: Automatic deletion
  • Today's Fortune / Today's Tarot / Constellation Fortune / Zodiac Fortune: Deleted after 3 days
  • This Month's Tarot: Deleted after 40 days

5. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

The Company does not provide users' personal information to third parties.

6. Outsourcing of Personal Information Processing

The Company outsources personal information processing as follows to provide smooth services:

Service Provider Outsourced Tasks
Firebase (Google LLC) Data storage, authentication, server operation, analytics

The Company stipulates necessary matters in accordance with applicable laws to ensure personal information is safely managed when entering outsourcing contracts.

7. Installation, Operation, and Rejection of Automatic Personal Information Collection Devices

The Company operates the following automatic collection devices:

Advertising Service: Google AdMob

  • Advertising ID may be collected for ad delivery purposes.

Analytics Service: Firebase Analytics

  • Log collection for app usage statistics and service improvement

Users can restrict or reset advertising ID usage through their device settings.

8. Procedure and Method of Personal Information Destruction

  • Electronic file format: Permanently deleted using non-recoverable methods
  • Paper document format: Shredded or incinerated

9. Technical and Administrative Measures for Personal Information Protection

The Company takes the following measures to protect personal information:

  • Minimizing personal information access rights
  • Data encryption and use of secure servers
  • Regular security inspections

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be modified in accordance with changes in laws or services. Any changes will be notified through in-app announcements or separate notices.
